## ![Bathroom](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/media/english/fullsize/b/bat/bathr/bathroom.png "bathroom")

1. bathroom cabinet: 浴室櫃
2. mixer tap: 混合式水龍頭
3. [beaker(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/beaker)
   1. (British English) a cup, usually made of plastic and often without a handle, used for drinking from
      1. She drank from a plastic beaker.
   2. (British English) the amount contained in a beaker
      1. a beaker of coffee
   3. a glass cup with straight sides and a lip, used in chemistry, for example for measuring liquids
      1. a glass beaker containing copper sulphate solution
4. [washbasin(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/washbasin)
    > also **basin**  
    > both especially British English  
    > also **sink** North American English, British English  
    > also especially North American English **washbowl**
   1. a large bowl that has taps and is fixed to the wall in a bathroom, used for washing your hands and face in
      1. She filled the washbasin with warm soapy water.
5. shower cubicle: 淋浴間
6. shower head: 淋浴噴頭
7. soap dish: 肥皂盤
8. shower tray: 淋浴盤
9. [plughole/drain(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/plughole)
   > (British English)  
   > (US English **drain**)
   1. a hole in a bath, sink, etc. where the water flows away and into which a plug fits
10. [towel rail/rack(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/towel-rail)
    > (British English)  
    > (also **towel rack**, both North American English)  
    1. a bar or frame for hanging towels on in a bathroom
       1. a heated towel rail
11. hand towel: 手巾
12. bath towel: 浴巾
13. bathroom scales: 浴室體重計
14. [bath mat(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/bath-mat)
    1. a piece of material that you put next to the bath to stand on when you get out
    2. a piece of rubber that you put on the bottom of the bath so that you do not slip
15. [bath/bathtub(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/bath_1)
    1. [countable] (British English) (also **bathtub**, informal **tub** North American English, British English)  
    a large, long container that you put water in and then get into to wash your whole body
       1. a bath with shower attachment
16. [tiling(noun, uncountable)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/tiling)
    1. an area covered with tiles
    2. the work of covering a floor, wall, etc. with tiles
17. [bathrobe(noun, also **robe**)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/bathrobe)
    1. a loose piece of clothing worn before and after taking a bath
    2. (North American English, British English **dressing gown**)  
    a long loose piece of clothing, usually with a belt, worn indoors over night clothes, for example when you first get out of bed
18. [bidet(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/bidet)
    1. a low bowl in the bathroom, usually with taps, that you fill with water and sit on to wash your bottom
19. [toilet(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/toilet)
    1. [countable] a large bowl attached to a pipe that you sit on or stand over when you get rid of waste matter from your body
       1. Have you flushed the toilet?
    2. (British English, North American English **bathroom**, countable) a room containing a toilet
       1. There was a communal toilet on the landing for the four flats.
    3. (British English, countable, also **toiilets** [plural]) a room or small building containing several toilets, each in a separate smaller room
       1. Could you tell me where the ladies' toilet is, please?
    4. (uncountable, old-fashioned) the process of washing and dressing yourself, arranging your hair, etc.
20. [cistern(noun)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/cistern)
    1. a container in which water is stored in a building, especially one in the roof or connected to a toilet
21. [toilet paper(noun, also **toilet tissue**, uncountable)](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/toilet-paper)
    1. thin, soft paper used for cleaning yourself after you have used the toilet
       1. a roll of toilet paper
